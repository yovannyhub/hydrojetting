Frequently Asked Questions

Hydro jetting typically ranges from $995–$1500 for residential services, depending on the severity of the blockage and pipe accessibility. We provide free camera inspections and upfront pricing before any work begins—no hidden fees.
Our trained technicians assess your pipes with a camera inspection first. We adjust the pressure (3000–5000 PSI) based on your pipe material and condition. Hydro jetting is safe for most pipes when performed by professionals.
Most residential hydro jetting jobs take 1–2 hours, including the initial camera inspection and final verification. Commercial properties may take longer depending on the scope of work.
Snaking only pokes a hole through the clog, providing a temporary fix. Hydro jetting uses high-pressure water to scour the entire interior of the pipe, removing all buildup including grease, roots, and scale for long-lasting results.
Hydro jetting is safe for PVC, cast iron, clay, and most modern pipe materials. Our technicians always perform a camera inspection first to assess pipe condition and choose the appropriate pressure level.
