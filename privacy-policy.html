Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Aurora Sewer Cleaning Corp ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or contact us for services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide, such as name, phone number, email address, and service address.
  • Appointment and service request details submitted through forms.
  • Basic technical data, such as browser type, pages visited, and general usage analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and schedule appointments.
  • To provide and improve drain and sewer services.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business (for example, scheduling or hosting platforms), and only as needed to provide services.

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information. No online transmission is 100% secure, but we take commercially reasonable steps to protect your data.

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies for site performance, analytics, and user experience improvements.

You may contact us to request updates or corrections to your personal information, or to ask questions about how your information is used.
